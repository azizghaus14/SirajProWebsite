4. Accuracy of prayer times

Siraj Pro calculates prayer times using established astronomical algorithms (Muslim World League by default, with optional ISNA, Egyptian, Umm al-Qura, and Karachi methods) based on your device's location and clock. While we strive for accuracy, the displayed times are approximations. You remain responsible for verifying prayer times with your local mosque or religious authority, especially in edge cases such as high-latitude regions.

5. The Qibla compass

The Qibla compass relies on your device's magnetometer and GPS. Accuracy can be affected by nearby metal objects, electronic interference, or an uncalibrated sensor. The compass is a helpful aid, not a substitute for physical reference or local guidance.
